About Me

I’ve been obsessed with building and flying drones since I was a teenager. What started as tinkering with cheap toy quads in my garage quickly turned into designing and hand-assembling some of the fastest FPV racing drones and most reliable cinematic rigs on the planet.

In 2018 I officially founded SKY SAVER — turning a bedroom passion project into a full-time business that now ships custom drones to pilots and filmmakers all over the world.

Every frame, every motor mount, and every flight controller board is designed and tested by me personally. I still solder, tune PIDs, and fly every single prototype myself because nothing beats the feeling of a perfectly balanced machine ripping through the sky at 150+ km/h.

Whether you need an ultra-light 5" freestyle beast, a long-range 7" explorer, or a buttery-smooth cinematic rig with zero prop wash — I build drones that don’t just fly… they dominate.

Welcome to my world. Let’s build something insane together.
